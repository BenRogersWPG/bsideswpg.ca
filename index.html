BSides Winnipeg 2013

BSides Winnipeg

November 16th & 17th in Winnipeg, Canada!

Where?

The King's Head Pub
120 King Street, Winnipeg, MB, Canada

When?

November 16th & 17th, 2013, from Noon to 7pm.

Cost?

Admission is $20. This also gets you 3 drink tickets!

Who?

BSides Winnipeg Inc. is a non-profit corporation established in July 2012 with the sole purpose of putting on BSides Winnipeg events. All conference expenses are being covered by our amazing sponsors and all the organization, planning, and execution is carried out by a dedicated group of volunteers with a desire to see this event happen in Winnipeg.

What?

Security B-Sides is an information security conference by the community for the community. It follows a traditional conference format but encourages participation, discussion, and collaboration.
